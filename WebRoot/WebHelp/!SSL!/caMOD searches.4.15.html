Detail Page
The top of the detail page displays the NSC# and a hypertext link to chemical data for the compound.
The detail page also lists in table format each individual experiment performed with the specified drug on the specific model. The table categories are described in the following table. Examples are given, where appropriate.
 
Administration of Drugs
Description
Schedule
Treatment Schedule
Example: Q04DX005 = total of 5 injections administered every four days
Q = every
04 = treatment interval
D = interval unit (minutes, hours, days)
X = times
005 = number of injections associated with the basic schedule
Route
Administration site of the drug
Vehicle
Medium in which the drug was diluted
Toxicity Results
 
Dose
Amount of drug administered
Toxicity Evaluation Day
A day specified by the screener that will serve as a toxicity valuation point for the compound under test.
Toxicity Survivors
The number of survivors on toxicity day and the total number of animals started on test.
Experiment Result
 
Endpoint
Parameter for measuring the success
Evaluation Day
Day on which the success of the experiment was evaluated
Treated/Controls (%)
Test evaluation divided by the control evaluation yield percent evaluation. Used to determine activity of test compound. The mean/median tumor weight or the mean/median survival time of the treated group is divided by that of the untreated group and multiplied by 100.
 
Example 1: Endpoint parameter is mean survival time:
treated animals survived 45 days
control animals survived 40 days
Calculation: 45 / 40 * 100 = 112.5%
 
Example 2: Endpoint parameter is mean tumor weight:
tumors of treated animals weight 10 grams
tumors of treated animals weight 12 grams
Calculation: 10 / 12 * 100 = 83%
