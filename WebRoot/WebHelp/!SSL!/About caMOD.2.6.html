User Settings
Once you log into caMOD, a list of links appears on the left sidebar of the Submit and Edit page. Your current login information and user permissions are listed at the bottom of this list.
1.
Click the User Settings link to open the User Settings dialog box where you can review and/or modify your registered user information.
2.
Click Submit to enter the new information. Click Cancel to abort the operation.
