Drug Screening Search
The Drug Screening Search page allows you to search for a specific compound or drug and results of its use in yeast screening, in-vivo screening, pre-clinical trials and in clinical trials. The yeast and in vivo screening data were obtained from the NCI Developmental Therapeutics Program
(http://dtp.nci.nih.gov).
